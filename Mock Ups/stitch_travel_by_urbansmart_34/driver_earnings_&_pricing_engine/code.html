Frequently Asked Questions

When do I get paid? expand_more
Payouts are processed weekly on Tuesdays. Instant cash-out is available for Gold tier drivers for a small fee.
What does the Platform Fee cover? expand_more
The platform fee goes towards app development, 24/7 driver support, marketing to get you more riders, and commercial insurance coverage during trips.
How is the Base Route Price calculated? expand_more
It is calculated based on the estimated distance (km) and time (minutes) of the most efficient route, plus a base flag-fall fee.
