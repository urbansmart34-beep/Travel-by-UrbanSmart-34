Pricing & Fees

How are fares calculated for long-distance travel?

expand_more

Fares are calculated based on a combination of base rate, distance (per km), and estimated time. For inter-city travel (e.g., Johannesburg to Durban), dynamic pricing may apply during peak holiday seasons. All estimated costs are shown upfront before you confirm your booking.

Are there booking fees or hidden costs?

expand_more

We believe in transparency. A small service fee is included in the final price shown to you to cover platform maintenance. Any tolls or airport surcharges will be itemized on your receipt.

Do you offer discounts for students or seniors?

expand_more

Yes! Verified students and seniors (60+) can apply for a 10% discount on standard economy rides. Please upload your ID or student card in the 'Account Settings' section to activate this benefit.

cancel

Cancellations

What is the refund policy?

expand_more

If you cancel more than 24 hours before your scheduled trip, you will receive a full refund to your original payment method. Cancellations made between 24 hours and 2 hours before the trip are eligible for a 50% refund or full credit towards a future ride.

Can I change my ride time instead of cancelling?

expand_more

Absolutely. You can reschedule your ride up to 1 hour before departure through the "My Trips" section in the app without incurring a cancellation fee. Fare differences may apply if the new time slot is during peak hours.

Requesting Rides

Is UrbanSmart-34 available in Cape Town?

expand_more

Yes, we operate fully in Cape Town, Johannesburg, Durban, and Pretoria. We are also expanding to the Garden Route and select Winelands areas later this year.

How do I book a ride in advance?

expand_more

To schedule a ride, select the "Schedule" icon (clock symbol) on the home screen instead of "Ride Now". You can book up to 30 days in advance to ensure availability for airport transfers or important meetings.
